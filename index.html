About Me

Abdi Kumsa Milkesa is a Computer Science and Engineering student at Adama Science and Technology University, driven by a passion for problem-solving, technology, and knowledge-sharing.

He founded an ICT Club in high school, training 80+ students and guiding 7 of them to earn acceptance into the AddisCoder program. He ranked among the top 0.025% of students nationwide with a score of 622/700.

At ASTU, he continued mentoring fellow students while also entering professional work with Rubi Pictures. He contributed to CGTN and IFRC Zero Hunger documentary projects, transcribing over 15+ hours of audio.

His consistency, communication, and reasoning earned him a promotion to Virtual Assistant, and later he became a co-founder with company ownership.
